About Basic Upper Primary school

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Basic Upper Primary school has been a beacon of educational excellence for over three decades. What started as a small institution with just 50 students has grown into a prestigious academy serving over 1,200 students from diverse backgrounds.

Our commitment to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character has made us one of the region's most respected educational institutions. We believe in preparing students not just for exams, but for life itself.

Located in the heart of the city, our modern campus provides state-of-the-art facilities including science laboratories, computer centers, sports complexes, and creative arts studios.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to become confident, capable, and caring individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that inspires lifelong learning, innovation, and leadership in our students.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors
  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and moral principles

  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and new ideas
  • Community: Building strong relationships and partnerships
